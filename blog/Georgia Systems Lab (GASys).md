- mission statement
	**GASys — Georgia Systems Lab — is an independent engineering research group dedicated to designing, building, and open-sourcing advanced systems in electronics, robotics, and additive manufacturing. Our mission is to push practical innovation forward by developing high-performance hardware that is accessible, reproducible, and engineered with academic rigor.**

	We combine first-principles engineering, modern fabrication tools, and rapid experimental iteration to create systems that are reliable, efficient, and scalable. GASys exists to empower students, researchers, and engineers by providing high-quality open-source designs, detailed documentation, and a community focused on learning through real hardware.

	**We aim to bridge the gap between academic research and hands-on engineering**, producing projects and technologies that can serve as educational resources, research platforms, and springboards for future development.
